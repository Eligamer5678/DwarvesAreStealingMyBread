## Recipes used in game
Note: recipes are not exact (unless ingredient amounts listed), but instead give the general idea. 

- Chocolate chip cookies
1. Ingredients
 - 1 cup Butter
 - 1 cup sugar
 - 1 cup light brown sugar
 - 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
 - 2 large eggs
 - 3 cups all purpose flour
 - 1 teaspoon basking soda
 - 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
 - 2 cups chocolate chips
2. Preheat oven to 375
3. Line pans with baking sheets
4. Beat eggs & vanilla in a large bowl
5. Slowly mix in the dry ingredients
6. Add chocolate chips
7. Roll 2-3 tablespoons of dough, evenly spaced.
8. Bake for 8-10 minutes, take out when starting to turn brown.
